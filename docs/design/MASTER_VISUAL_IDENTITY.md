# VISUAL RULES & GUIDELINES

### BACKGROUND
* Soft pearl gradients (#FDFCFB to #F8F6F4).
* Warm white surfaces with very subtle ambient glows.
* No dark modes (unless requested for specific night-flow modules).

### CARDS (PANELS)
* Translucent ivory and frosted glass.
* Extremely soft shadows (Ivory/Lavender tints).
* Large border radius (`28px`) for a soft, friendly but premium feel.

### SPACING
* "Gallery Spacing": Breathable, generous luxury whitespace.

### TYPOGRAPHY
* Editorial hierarchy: 
  - Headings: Elegant sans-serif (Inter/Outfit), light weights (200-300).
  - Content: Clear, sophisticated dark gray text (#333).
  - Accents: Small caps for labels, tracking increased.

### BUTTONS
* Refined pill shapes.
* Subtle pearl-to-pink gradients.
* Invisible states that appear on hover.
